Restaurants by Zipcode

Potluck is built on the Zomato API database, which calls over 1.5 million restuarants globally. Unlike crowdsourced reviews apps, we don't allow restaurants to influence the order in which restaurants appear.

Sorted by News Coverage

Potluck scans over 30,000 news sources for the latest published on given restaurants in your zipcode, so you can see who gets the best press (and the worse) in one click. Access news articles from the homescreen.

Potluck Shortcut

Potluck is your shortcut to a great time out. Finally, a single place to find the hottest trending restaurants near you, without having to sort through confusing reviews systems.

Our Team
